# 环境动态词汇库

## 风相关动态

**微风（Subtle Breeze）**
- hair gently swaying in breeze：头发在微风中轻轻摆动
- leaves rustling softly：树叶轻柔沙沙作响
- fabric slightly billowing：布料轻微鼓起
- wind chimes tinkling gently：风铃轻轻叮当

**中风（Moderate Wind）**
- clothes flapping moderately：衣服适度飘动
- tree branches swaying：树枝摇摆
- curtains flowing inward：窗帘向内飘动
- papers scattering on desk：桌上纸张散落

**强风（Strong Wind）**
- hair whipping across face：头发抽打面部
- clothes flapping vigorously：衣服剧烈飘动
- debris flying through air：碎片在空中飞舞
- trees bending under force：树木在风力下弯曲

## 水相关动态

**静水动态**
- ripples spreading across water surface：涟漪在水面扩散
- concentric circles expanding：同心圆扩大
- reflections distorting gently：倒影轻微扭曲
- water droplets creating interference patterns：水滴产生干涉图案

**流水动态**
- water flowing over rocks：水流过岩石
- stream bubbling around obstacles：溪流绕过障碍物冒泡
- current creating whirlpools：水流形成漩涡
- waterfall cascading down：瀑布倾泻而下

**水花动态**
- water droplets splashing upward：水滴向上飞溅
- splash crown forming upon impact：撞击形成水花冠
- spray misting the air：水雾喷洒到空中
- puddle ripples from raindrops：雨滴在水坑中产生涟漪

## 光影动态

**自然光变化**
- sunlight patches slowly moving across floor：阳光斑块缓慢移动
- shadows gradually lengthening：阴影逐渐拉长
- light intensity shifting with cloud cover：光线强度随云层变化
- golden hour light warming the scene：黄金时段光线温暖场景

**人造光变化**
- neon sign flickering intermittently：霓虹灯牌间歇闪烁
- candle flame dancing and casting moving shadows：烛焰舞动并投射移动阴影
- flashlight beam scanning across area：手电筒光束扫过区域
- projector light cycling through colors：投影仪光线循环变色

## 粒子效果

**自然粒子**
- dust particles floating in sunlight beam：尘埃颗粒在阳光光束中漂浮
- pollen drifting through air：花粉在空气中飘散
- sand grains being lifted by wind：沙粒被风扬起
- snowflakes falling at varying speeds：雪花以不同速度飘落

**人工粒子**
- sparks flying from impact：撞击产生的火花飞溅
- embers rising from fire：余烬从火中升起
- confetti floating downward：五彩纸屑向下飘落
- glitter catching the light：闪粉捕捉光线
