# 模块填写指南

## 1. 镜头运动模块

**格式：** `[运动类型] [方向/方式] [速度修饰]`

**示例：**
- `Slow dolly in with subtle pan to the right`
- `Quick zoom out while panning left`
- `Smooth orbit around subject`

**限制：** 最多2种运动组合

## 2. 主体动作模块

**格式：** `[主体] [主要动作] [伴随动作]`

**示例：**
- `Subject turns head while raising hand slightly`
- `Character jumps backward with arms outstretched`
- `Protagonist reaches for object while maintaining eye contact`

**限制：** 主要动作1个，次要动作可选1个

## 3. 环境动态模块

**格式：** `[环境元素] [动态类型] [强度修饰]`

**示例：**
- `Hair flowing gently in breeze with dust particles floating in sunlight`
- `Clothing flaps vigorously and debris flies upward`
- `Leaves rustling softly as raindrops begin to fall`

**要求：** 必须包含，至少1个环境动态

## 4. 节奏控制模块

**格式：** `[速度梯度] [时间分配] [节奏点]`

**示例：**
- `Gradual acceleration over 2 seconds, hold for 1 second, then rapid deceleration`
- `Sudden burst of speed, sustained for 0.5 seconds, then rapid decay`
- `Slow and steady pace throughout, with subtle pauses for emphasis`

**要求：** 明确时间比例和速度变化

## 5. 氛围强化模块

**格式：** `[风格化效果] [技术参数] [情绪强化]`

**示例：**
- `Cinematic 24fps with subtle motion blur and film grain`
- `Dynamic action shot with exaggerated motion blur and lens flare`
- `Dreamy slow motion with soft focus and bokeh highlights`

**可选但建议包含**
